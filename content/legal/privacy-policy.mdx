# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2024
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit TheStatsCalculator, we automatically collect certain information to provide and improve our services:

#### Device and Browser Information
- **Browser Type**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.
- **Operating System**: Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android
- **Device Type**: Desktop, tablet, mobile phone
- **Screen Resolution**: For responsive design optimization
- **Language Preferences**: For content localization

#### Usage Data
- **Pages Visited**: Which calculators and pages you access
- **Time Spent**: Duration of visits to different sections
- **Calculator Usage**: Which statistical tools you use and how often
- **Error Reports**: Technical issues encountered during use

#### Technical Data
- **IP Address**: For general location and security purposes
- **Browser Headers**: HTTP request information
- **Referral Sources**: How you found our website
- **Device Fingerprinting**: For security and fraud prevention

### Personally Identifiable Information

We collect information you voluntarily provide when interacting with our services:

#### Contact Information
- **Email Address**: When you contact us or subscribe to updates
- **Name**: When you choose to identify yourself
- **Organization**: When you represent a business or institution

#### Usage Preferences
- **Calculator Settings**: Saved preferences and default values
- **Calculation History**: Previously performed calculations (if saved)
- **Custom Inputs**: Personal data you enter into calculators

#### Feedback and Contributions
- **Comments**: Feedback on tools and content
- **Suggestions**: Ideas for new features or improvements
- **Bug Reports**: Technical issues you encounter
- **Content Contributions**: Educational materials you share

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for specific, legitimate purposes:

### Service Provision
- **Provide Calculators**: Deliver statistical calculation tools
- **Process Requests**: Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- **Improve Performance**: Optimize loading times and user experience
- **Fix Issues**: Identify and resolve technical problems

### User Experience Enhancement
- **Personalization**: Customize content and tool recommendations
- **Usage Analytics**: Understand how people use our calculators
- **Feature Development**: Prioritize new tools based on user needs
- **Content Optimization**: Improve educational materials and guides

### Communication
- **Support Responses**: Answer your questions and provide assistance
- **Service Updates**: Notify you about new features or maintenance
- **Educational Content**: Share relevant statistical tips and resources
- **Feedback Follow-up**: Respond to your suggestions and contributions

### Legal and Security
- **Compliance**: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detect and prevent malicious activities
- **Security Protection**: Safeguard our services and user data
- **Terms Enforcement**: Ensure compliance with our terms of service

## Information Sharing

We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not sell your personal information.

### When We May Share Information

#### Service Providers
We may share information with third-party service providers who:
- **Host Our Services**: Cloud infrastructure and web hosting
- **Process Payments**: Financial transaction processing
- **Send Communications**: Email delivery and newsletter services
- **Analyze Data**: Analytics and user behavior research

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

#### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when:
- **Required by Law**: Responding to legal processes and government requests
- **Protect Rights**: Defending our rights, property, or safety
- **Prevent Harm**: Preventing fraud, security threats, or illegal activities
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

#### With Your Consent
We may share information when you explicitly authorize it:
- **Public Profiles**: Information you choose to make public
- **Social Sharing**: Content you share on social media platforms
- **Third-party Services**: When you connect with external applications

### Data We Never Share
- **Calculation Inputs**: Personal data entered into our calculators
- **Individual Usage Patterns**: Detailed tracking of your specific activities
- **Contact Information**: Without your explicit consent
- **Identifiable Data**: In aggregated analytics reports

## Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

### Technical Security
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Secure Storage**: Sensitive data encrypted at rest
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal information
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Backup Systems**: Regular data backups with secure storage

### Organizational Security
- **Employee Training**: Regular privacy and security awareness training
- **Access Policies**: Strict access control and authentication requirements
- **Data Minimization**: Collect only necessary information
- **Retention Policies**: Regular review and deletion of unnecessary data
- **Incident Response**: Procedures for handling security breaches

### User Security
- **No Account Required**: Most features work without creating an account
- **Local Processing**: Calculations performed in your browser when possible
- **Anonymous Usage**: No tracking of individual users without consent
- **Privacy Controls**: Options to manage your privacy preferences
