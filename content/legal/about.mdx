# About TheStatsCalculator

## Mission

At TheStatsCalculator, we believe that statistical analysis should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide powerful, easy-to-use calculators and educational resources that help students, researchers, and professionals make data-driven decisions.

## What We Do

We develop comprehensive statistical tools that cover:

- **Descriptive Statistics**: Mean, median, mode, standard deviation, and more
- **Inferential Statistics**: Confidence intervals, hypothesis testing
- **Educational Resources**: Step-by-step guides, tutorials, and glossaries
- **Real-world Applications**: Case studies and practical examples

## Our Team

We're a team of statisticians, educators, and software developers passionate about making statistics more approachable.

### Our Values

- **Accessibility**: Tools should be easy to use and understand
- **Accuracy**: Calculations must be precise and reliable
- **Education**: Learning is as important as getting results
- **Community**: Building a supportive space for statistical learning
